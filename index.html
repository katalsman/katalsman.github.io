About

Linda Scott - Principal

Founded by Linda Scott in 1979, Scott Design provides clients with quality residential design. Our projects range from interior furnishings to the complete design of new homes. We can provide a complete project package including, but not limited to architectural and engineering coordination, historic preservation services and permit processing. Scott Design’s signature philosophy is to create a quality product for the client, the situation and the budget. Quality begins with awareness of scale, style and use; it ends with careful attention to detail that reflects resounding quality. We generally design from the inside out with an understanding of how the spaces are to be used. We then establish a theme appropriate to the setting and the client’s needs.
